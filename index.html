Home

I am a doctoral student at Virginia Tech planning to graduate by Early Fall 2021. I work with Prof. Jonathan Boreyko in Nature-Inspired Fluids and Interfaces Lab. My research projects are centered around Interfacial and Phase-change Phenomena, e.g., jumping droplet condensation, liquid-infused surfaces and physicochemical phenomena involving frost. Before coming to USA, I completed my undergraduate Mechanical Engineering education at Jadavpur University in my hometown, Kolkata, India. I also spent 2 years in industry designing thermal heaters for petrochemical industries.
I am an amateur home cook and photographer. Feel free to check my Flickr® account for the clicks.
